A game by Daniel Monteiro.
Art by Stone Soup project.

Controls:
- arrows to move and turn.
- z and x to strafe left and right.
- s (or 1 - 3) to cycle the inventory.
- a to pick items.
- space to use the current item.
- esc to quit (but why?!).
